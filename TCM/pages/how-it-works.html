How it Works

Here's how to get started. First, choose 1-3 "Tiny Changes", or microhabits, that you want to adopt. We'll check in with you every day for 30 days* to see if you've done your tiny change for the day. Every day that you've completed your tiny change, you get a gold star. (Yep, just like in grade school. They were fun to get then, and they're still fun to get!) After 30 days, your tiny change is now part of your life and you can choose some more - good job you! *Why 30 days? Learn more here.
